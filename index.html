Echoel Studio

Echoel Studio is a software for multidimensional media and event production. Build your professional workstation with a modular plugin system - from the first idea to the online presentation.

Features

Intuitive User Interface

Designed for a smooth workflow, from the first draft to the finished product.

AI-Powered Tools

  • Harmonic Analysis: Analyze and understand the harmonic structure of your music.
  • Music Style Database: Access a constantly updated database of music styles and composition techniques.
  • Voice Synthesis Avatars: Create unique voice synthesis avatars with songwriting capabilities via MIDI.

Spatial Audio-Visual Control

Control sounds and visuals spatially in real-time using your mobile device via WebRTC.

Biofeedback Integration

Integrate biofeedback data from EEG, HRV devices, and even your front camera lens to create interactive experiences.

Plugin Suite for all major DAWs

Our plugins offer:

  • Creative Sound Design: Unique effects and modulations.
  • Precise Audio Production: Professional tools for mixing and mastering.
  • Seamless Integration: Use directly within your DAW.

Octave-Analog Visual Mapping

Map audio and biofeedback data to the visual realm using octave-analog translation.

Comprehensive Production Tools

  • Spatial Audio and Video Production: Produce immersive experiences.
  • Streaming and Rendering: Stream and render high-quality content.
  • Image Editing: Edit and manipulate images.

Digital Distribution and Social Media Management

Manage your content distribution and social media presence.
