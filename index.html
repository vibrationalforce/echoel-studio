Echoel Studio

Echoel Studio is a software for multidimensional media and event production. Build your professional workstation with a modular plugin system - from the first idea to the online presentation. Be inspired by unusual instruments and effects, AI tools, spatial audio-visual tools, scientific background knowledge, and biofeedback.

Features

Intuitive User Interface

Designed for a smooth workflow, from the first draft to the finished product.

Powerful Tools

Professional tools for sound design, audio production, and more.

Extensive Plugin Support

Compatible with a wide range of plugins for extended functionality.

Plugin Suite for all major DAWs

Seamlessly integrate Echoel Studio into your preferred DAW and use its full power.
Our plugins offer:

  • Creative Sound Design:

    Modify and shape sounds with unique effects and modulations.

  • Precise Audio Production:

    Optimize your tracks with professional tools for mixing and mastering.

  • Seamless Integration:

    Use our plugins directly in your DAW, without additional software.

  • High-quality Instruments, Drums, and Effects:

    Create professional productions with high-quality virtual instruments, realistic drums, and versatile effects.

  • Extensive Live Features:

    Use exceptional timing and pitching settings for unique live performances.

  • Biofeedback to Audio Visual Option:

    Connect your performance with unique visual effects based on your biofeedback.

  • Control via Mobile Browser WebRTC:

    Control Echoel Studio conveniently via your mobile browser using WebRTC.

  • Octave-Analog Visualization:

    Experience your music in real-time with various design options.

  • Spatial Audio and Video Production as well as Streaming and Rendering:

    Produce immersive spatial audio and video content for streaming and rendering.

  • Image Editing:

    Edit your images directly in Echoel Studio.

  • AI-powered Features:

    Utilize innovative AI features for automatic mixing and mastering tasks and much more.
